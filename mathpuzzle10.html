Calculation 10

v1.2.0 — Brain Training Puzzle for iOS

Download on the App Store

Overview

Calculation 10 (MathPuzzle10) is a brain-training puzzle where you use four numbers and arithmetic operators (+, -, ×, ÷) to make 10. Simple rules, but it takes creative thinking and mathematical intuition to find every solution.

How to Play

  1. Look at the four numbers on screen
  2. Build an equation using operators (+, -, ×, ÷) and parentheses
  3. Make the result equal to 10
  4. Correct answer: +1 point, +10 seconds bonus
  5. Skip a problem: -5 seconds penalty
  6. Timer starts at 90 seconds — game over when it hits 0

Game Rules

  • Time limit: starts at 90 seconds
  • Scoring: +1 point and +10 seconds for each correct answer
  • Skip: -5 seconds penalty to move to the next problem
  • Operators: +, -, ×, ÷, and parentheses are allowed
  • Rule: each number must be used exactly once
  • Decimals: intermediate decimals are fine as long as the final result is 10

Examples

Numbers: 2, 3, 4, 1

Solution: 2 + 3 + 4 + 1 = 10

Numbers: 1, 2, 3, 6

Solution: 1 × 2 + (3 - 1) × 6 ÷ ... — multiple valid solutions exist!

Features

  • Infinite problems — procedurally generated so you never run out
  • Hint system — get a nudge when you're stuck
  • Multiple solutions — many problems have more than one valid answer
  • Clean, minimal design — stay focused on the puzzle
  • Level system — difficulty scales with your progress

FAQ

Can I use the same number twice?

No. Each number must be used exactly once.

What if I can't solve a problem?

Tap Skip to move on. It costs 5 seconds.

Are decimal answers accepted?

Yes, as long as the final result equals 10.

What version is this?

Current version: v1.2.0 (iOS)
