Calculation Card Game

v1.1.1 — Math Puzzle for iOS

Download on the App Store

Overview

Calculation Card Game is a math puzzle where you combine number and operator cards (+, -, ×, ÷) to hit the target number. Simple rules, but you'll need strategic thinking and quick calculation to master it.

How to Play

  1. Check the target number displayed at the top of the screen
  2. Select numbers and operators from the 16 cards to build an equation
  3. Tap the calculate button to check your answer
  4. Correct answers advance you to the next round — solve as many as you can before time runs out!

Features

  • Progressive difficulty — target range increases as you solve more problems
  • Shuffle — stuck? Rearrange the cards and discover new combinations
  • High score tracking — beat your personal best and watch your skills improve
  • Synthwave visuals — stylish neon-inspired design
  • Sound effects & BGM — immersive audio that keeps you in the zone

Difficulty Progression

  • Problems 1-5: target range 1 – 20
  • Problems 6-10: target range 20 – 60
  • Problems 11-15: target range 60 – 120
  • Problem 16+: target range 1 – 200
